Velona's Jungle Tips

We're happy to have you here! Enjoy Florence like a local with our favorite places to eat, drink, and explore — and don't miss our lovely walks starting from Piazza Goldoni, perfect to discover the city's hidden gems.




We love starting our walks along the river, on Lungarno Vespucci.

From there, we reach Piazza Goldoni, where we usually decide which way to go…

Here are five of our favorite walks (all starting from Piazza Goldoni):



  1. Follow the river to reach Ponte Vecchio, the Uffizi Gallery, and Piazza della Signoria.

  2. Head toward the Duomo (Cathedral of Santa Maria del Fiore) through Via della Vigna Nuova, crossing Via Tornabuoni and Piazza Strozzi.

  3. Cross Ponte alla Carraia to explore the Oltrarno area, passing through Via di Santo Spirito, Piazza Santo Spirito, and Piazza Pitti.

  4. Enjoy the beauty of Piazza Santa Maria Novella, walking along Via dei Fossi (the street of antique dealers) and visiting the beautiful Santa Maria Novella Pharmacy - Officina del Profumo.

  5. For one of the best views over Florence, cross Ponte Vecchio, walk up Costa San Giorgio, and enter the stunning Villa Bardini Gardens.
