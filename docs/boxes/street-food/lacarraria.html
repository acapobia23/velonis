Gelataria la Carraia

A historic artisanal gelato shop located near Ponte alla Carraia, the second‑oldest bridge in Florence. Loved by both tourists and locals for its creamy texture and inventive flavors like ricotta & pear and classic stracciatella.

They use only fresh milk and cream, crafting quality gelato with natural ingredients since 1990. The atmosphere is bright and simple, with a display that highlights the colorful selection.

distance from Duomo : 950m

distance from Ponte Vechhio : 600m
