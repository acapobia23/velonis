Taxi and Uber in Florence

Taxis are licensed and can be found at stands near major landmarks or booked by phone or via apps like AppTaxi and TaxiMove, especially during high season (May–June and September–October) and at peak hours, such as around 10:00 AM when many hotel check-outs happen, Please note, however, that the two main taxi companies don’t always accept advance bookings at all times. In some cases, they may only allow you to call for a taxi at the exact moment you need it. This also depends on the season and the time of day. For advance bookings, sending a message via WhatsApp is often the most reliable and secure method.
They are reliable, but availability may vary, so it’s best to book in advance, especially early or late.

The Uber app works in Florence only for Uber Black (private NCC drivers), not standard ride‑share.
It offers up‑front booking and licensed drivers but is more expensive than taxis.



If you're unable to find a taxi, don’t forget you can also check the Private Van section on our website.

  • Taxi :

    Taxi in Florence – option 1
    In Florence, taxis cannot be hailed by raising your hand or whistling. For a licensed taxi, call +39 055 4242 or +39 055 4390 . They speak English and are reliable for city rides and airport transfers.

    Taxi in Florence – option 2
    You can also request a taxi via WhatsApp/SMS at +39 333 807 7555 or +39 334 662 2550. Especially useful for late‑night service.


  • Uber in Florence


    Uber works only with licensed private‑chauffeur cars (“Uber Black”), bookable via the app for a premium service.
