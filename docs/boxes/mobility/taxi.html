Taxi and Uber in Florence

Taxis are licensed and can be found at stands near major landmarks or booked by phone or via apps like AppTaxi and TaxiMove.
They are reliable, but availability may vary, so it’s best to book in advance, especially early or late.

The Uber app works in Florence only for Uber Black (private NCC drivers), not standard ride‑share.
It offers up‑front booking and licensed drivers but is more expensive than taxis.
