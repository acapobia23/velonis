Since its founding in 1981, Tenax has been the beating heart of Florence's underground music scene. Born during the rise of new wave, punk, and dark sounds from the UK, Tenax quickly became a creative hub for emerging artists, fashion designers, and musicians.



Today, Tenax is internationally renowned for its Saturday night residency "Nobody's Perfect", a Techhouse music ritual featuring top DJs from around the world. The club's legacy includes performances by iconic bands such as Spandau Ballet, New Order, and Tears for Fears.



Located in the Novoli district, Tenax offers a raw, industrial vibe with high-quality sound systems and a crowd that blends locals, creatives, and international music lovers.
