Weekly Tips

The Weekend - July 26-27 in Florence

THE WEEKEND 20-21th SEPTEMBER IN FLORENCE



EVENTS, MARKETS AND FOOD:



-

The Rose That Grew From Concrete

is a contemporary art group show held at the former Convent of Sant’Orsola.

Through site-specific works, fourteen international artists and artisans explore the transformation of the space, symbolically tend to its wounds, and pay tribute to the power of nature.

-

Creative Factory

Piazza Santissima Annunziata turns into a vibrant space full of inspiration: handmade jewelry, accessories, clothing, illustrations, ceramics, home decor, and more.

(9:30 AM – 8 PM)

-

Indipendenza Antiquaria

Piazza Indipendenza fills up with 110 stalls of antiques: furniture, books, prints, sculptures, collectibles, paintings, and more.

(9 AM – 7 PM)

-

Ars Manualis – The Legacy of Craftsmanship

In Piazza Santa Maria Novella, enjoy a weekend market showcasing traditional Tuscan artisanal work.

(10 AM – 7 PM)

-

Passione Vintage

Piazza dei Ciompi becomes a treasure trove of vintage finds: jewelry, clothes, accessories, bags, and antiques.

(9 AM – 7 PM)

-

Visarno Cosplay & Co

At the Visarno Racecourse (Le Cascine), dive into the colorful world of cosplay, comics, and games!

-

Cirk Fantastik

Also at Parco delle Cascine, this international contemporary circus festival features performances, concerts, workshops, and food.

-

Lebanese Food Festival

At the Limonaia of Villa Strozzi, explore the rich and fragrant flavors of Middle Eastern cuisine all weekend long.

-

Santo Spirito in Fiera

On Sunday, Piazza Santo Spirito hosts a vibrant market with artistic crafts, antiques, and delicious food.

(8 AM – 8 PM)

-

Only Usato Market

On Sunday at Frida al Piazzale (near Piazzale Michelangelo), browse vintage and second-hand clothes and home goods.

-

Vino al Vino

Head to Panzano in Chianti to taste old and new vintages of Chianti Classico, and meet local winemakers.

-

Fiori in Fiera

Piazza IV Novembre in Sesto Fiorentino hosts a flower and plant fair, with nurseries, farms, and florists from all over Tuscany.

-

Antique Markets

On Saturday, Empoli’s city center welcomes back its antique, vintage, and collectibles market.

On Sunday, the scene moves to Piazza Mino in Fiesole.

-

Sagre

In Il Girone – a hamlet of Fiesole – the Truffle Festival returns.

Pian di San Bartolo will instead host the Steak Festival.

Scarperia will celebrate the Tortello and Porcini Mushroom Festival, while in Pelago you’ll find the Tortello and Truffle Specialties Festival.

Finally, Fucecchio will host the Festival of the Traditional Local Cockerel.
