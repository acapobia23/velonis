Weekly Tips

The Weekend 11-12th October in Florence


This weekend Florence opens its doors to hidden treasures and celebrates
the best of autumn. From exclusive visits to normally closed locations,
to vibrant markets and seasonal festivals, October reveals the city's
most authentic and charming side. Get ready to explore, discover, and enjoy!



Markets, Museums and Events


FAI Autumn Days

This weekend, join special visits with a voluntary contribution to 700 amazing places
in over 350 Italian cities—many of them usually closed to the public or simply unusual,
curious, and often overlooked. A unique opportunity to discover Italy's hidden gems.

More info: www.giornatefai.it



Beato Angelico

Palazzo Strozzi and the San Marco Museum team up for a stunning exhibition dedicated
to Fra Giovanni da Fiesole, also known as Beato Angelico, a true icon of 15th-century
Florentine art. A rare opportunity to immerse yourself in Renaissance spirituality and beauty.



Toulouse-Lautrec, a Journey into Belle Époque Paris

The Innocenti Museum hosts a new temporary exhibition focused on one of the most iconic
artists of the Belle Époque: Henri de Toulouse-Lautrec. Step into the cabarets, cafés,
and bohemian spirit of turn-of-the-century Paris.



The Rose That Grew From Concrete

A contemporary art group show at the former Sant'Orsola Convent featuring 14 international
artists and artisans who created site-specific works telling the story of the place's
transformation and honoring the healing power of nature. A poetic dialogue between
art, architecture, and resilience.



Sunday on Paper

A special day to highlight the rich paper heritage stored in state libraries and archives.
The libraries at Palazzo Medici Riccardi—Riccardiana and Moreniana—will literally open
their doors to the public for a fascinating glimpse into centuries of written history.

Info & booking: info@palazzomediciriccardi.it



Piantala

The biggest pop-up plant sale lands at Manifattura Tabacchi from Friday to Sunday,
with over 15,000 plants and more than 200 varieties! Perfect for plant lovers and
anyone looking to bring a touch of green into their home.

Hours: 10:00 AM – 8:00 PM



Antiques in the City Center

On Saturday in Empoli, discover a great market of antiques, vintage items, collectibles,
and more. A treasure trove for collectors and enthusiasts of the past.

Hours: 8:30 AM – 7:30 PM



Creative Factory

Back in Piazza dei Ciompi—a market of modern craftwork featuring unique creations
by local designers, artists, and makers. Support local talent and find one-of-a-kind pieces.

Hours: 10:00 AM – 8:00 PM



Oltrarno Arts and Crafts

On Sunday in Piazza Santo Spirito, explore lots of stalls with handmade goods, antiques,
books, clothes, vintage finds, and local products like honey, truffles, and more.
A beloved neighborhood tradition celebrating artisanal craftsmanship.



Fierucolina in Piazza del Carmine

Sunday morning grocery shopping, Fierucola-style—with fresh bread, pasta, vegetables,
new olive oil and wine, fruit, honey, flours, and cheeses. Taste the authentic flavors
of Tuscan countryside brought directly to the heart of Florence.

Hours: 9:00 AM – 2:00 PM



Only Usato Market

On Sunday at Conventino Caffè Letterario, check out a second-hand market full of clothes,
accessories, and home items—all surrounded by plants, flowers, vegetables, and even chickens!
A quirky, green oasis in the heart of the city.

Hours: 11:00 AM – 7:00 PM



Firenze Book Festival

At the Visarno Hippodrome in the Cascine park, a festival all about independent bookstores,
books, and connecting readers with authors and industry folks. A celebration of literature
and the joy of reading.



Chestnut Festival

Fall in Tuscany means celebrating one of its seasonal stars: chestnuts! Head to San Piero
a Sieve, Marradi, or anywhere in the Mugello for chestnut treats in all kinds of delicious
forms. From roasted to desserts, a true taste of autumn.



Pumpkin Kingdom

At Villa Montalvo Park in Campi Bisenzio, you'll find a full-on pumpkin village—
an immersive experience celebrating this autumn favorite. Perfect for families and
anyone who loves the magic of the fall season.
