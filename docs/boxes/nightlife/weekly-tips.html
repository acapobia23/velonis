The Weekend 31st October - 2nd November in Florence


This weekend Florence celebrates Halloween traditions, fashion heritage, and autumn flavors.
From spooky dinners in historic villas to artisan markets, from contemporary art to seasonal tastings,
November begins with a perfect blend of culture, entertainment, and culinary experiences.
Get ready to explore, celebrate, and discover!
