Weekly Tips

The Weekend 15th-16th November in Florence


This weekend Florence continues to celebrate Renaissance masters and Belle Époque art,
while welcoming the festive season with Christmas markets and cultural events.
From Beato Angelico to Toulouse-Lautrec, from vintage treasures to solidarity initiatives,
the weekend offers culture, shopping, and seasonal delights.
Get ready to explore, discover, and enjoy!



Beato Angelico at Palazzo Strozzi

Fondazione Palazzo Strozzi and the Museo di San Marco present Beato Angelico,
an extraordinary exhibition dedicated to Fra Giovanni da Fiesole,
a true symbol of 15th-century Florentine art.



Toulouse-Lautrec, A Journey into the Paris of the Belle Époque

The Museo degli Innocenti hosts a new temporary exhibition exploring the life and work
of one of the most iconic artists of the Belle Époque --- Henri de Toulouse-Lautrec.



Oceans. David Doubilet at Villa Bardini

Villa Bardini celebrates the work of David Doubilet,
a pioneer of underwater exploration and one of the world's leading underwater photographers.



Christmas Market at Piazza Santa Croce

Discover the magic of Christmas in Piazza Santa Croce with the Weihnachtsmarkt, the traditional German Christmas market.
Every day you'll find stalls with crafts, typical products, gift ideas, Christmas decorations, and European food.

When: 10 AM - 10/11 PM



Tea with You at the Villa

Selected Made-in-Italy crafts in the beautiful 16th-century Villa Machiavelli Strozzi,
with handmade creations perfect for Christmas gifts.
In the afternoon, tea and cookies will be served, and you can also visit the 14th-century dè Lari Tower.

When: 10 AM - 7 PM



Christmas Bazaar at Visarno

At the Visarno racetrack you'll find Alio's market,
full of vintage and new clothing and accessories, jewelry, toys, books, Christmas decorations, handmade pieces ---
an event where solidarity meets Christmas magic!



Empoli, Christmas City

The historic center will host a big Christmas event with light installations, new attractions, and 70 market chalets!
Note that during the weekend, Piazza G. Guerra will also have "Vintage & Handmade Passion,"
a market dedicated to vintage and high-quality crafts.

When: 10 AM - 7 PM



The Legacy of Women

A festival dedicated to female empowerment and skills, with artistic director Serena Dandini.
A weekend full of important voices and guests (info on their website).



Solidarity Market at Piazza Tasso

In Piazza Tasso comes the charity second-hand market
that mixes the love for vintage and pre-loved objects with a good cause.

When: 10 AM - 6 PM



Emporium - Saturday 15th November

On Saturday, Piazza Savonarola will host an antiques and vintage market.

When: 9 AM - 6 PM



Only Second-Hand Market - Sunday 16th November

At the Limonaia of Villa Strozzi, Sunday brings back the event
dedicated to the culture of second-hand and vintage.

When: 11 AM - 7 PM



Santo Spirito Fair - Sunday 16th November

On Sunday you can shop for crafts, food products, wellness items, small antiques,
clothing, costume jewelry, and much more.

When: 9 AM - 7 PM
