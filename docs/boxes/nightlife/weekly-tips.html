Weekly Tips

The Weekend 15th-16th November in Florence


This weekend Florence continues to celebrate Renaissance masters and Belle Époque art,
while welcoming the festive season with Christmas markets and autumn traditions.
From Beato Angelico to Toulouse-Lautrec, from LEGO festivals to vintage treasures,
the third weekend of November offers culture, shopping, and seasonal delights.
Get ready to explore, discover, and enjoy!



Toulouse-Lautrec at Museo degli Innocenti

The Museo degli Innocenti hosts a new temporary exhibition exploring the life and work
of one of the most iconic artists of the Belle Époque: Henri de Toulouse-Lautrec.
A journey into the Paris of the Belle Époque.



Oceans. David Doubilet at Villa Bardini

Villa Bardini celebrates the work of David Doubilet,
a pioneer of underwater exploration and one of the world's leading underwater photographers.



Beato Angelico at Palazzo Strozzi

Fondazione Palazzo Strozzi and the Museo di San Marco present Beato Angelico,
an extraordinary exhibition dedicated to Fra Giovanni da Fiesole,
a true symbol of 15th-century Florentine art.



Prim.Olio

Extra-virgin olive oil takes center stage in Bagno a Ripoli,
with dozens of local producers offering tastings and the chance to buy their fresh-pressed "green gold".

When: 9 AM - 7 PM



Christmas Market at Piazza Santa Croce

Discover the magic of Christmas in Piazza Santa Croce with the Weihnachtsmarkt, the traditional German Christmas market.
Every day you'll find stalls with crafts, typical products, gift ideas, decorations, and European foods.

When: 10 AM - 10/11 PM



Bricks in Florence Festival

At Teatro Cartiere Carrara one of Italy's biggest LEGO events is back, dedicated to brick lovers of all ages.
From the moving funfair to the 70s Classic Space world, from One Piece pirates to settings inspired by Sherlock Holmes and Stranger Things.

When: 10 AM - 8 PM



Dal kitchs al kitchs

In the Parterre room in Piazza della Libertà you can take home all sorts of curiosities from the recent past
while supporting the fight against cancer --- original souvenirs, quirky jewelry and accessories, unique home décor pieces.



Passione Vintage - This Weekend

Weekend event in Piazza dei Ciompi for lovers of vintage objects and a pleasantly retro atmosphere, with dozens of stalls.

When: 9:30 AM - 8 PM



Indipendenza Antiquaria - This Weekend

This weekend Piazza Indipendenza hosts 110 stalls with furniture, books, prints, sculptures, antiques, paintings, and much more.

When: 9 AM - 7 PM



Fiera delle Cascine - Sunday 16th November

On Sunday the big autumn fair returns to Parco delle Cascine,
with hundreds of stalls selling everything from clothing to home goods to food and wine specialties.
Take the chance to hop on the 55-meter Ferris wheel and enjoy Florence from above!

When: 8 AM - 8 PM



Fierucola di Santo Spirito - Sunday 16th November

On Sunday you can shop for farm products and handmade crafts ---
a great mix of manual craftsmanship and organic agriculture.

When: 9 AM - 7 PM



Sapori e Colori - Sunday 16th November

On Sunday Piazza Mino in Fiesole hosts the market-fair of collectibles, crafts, and more.

When: 9 AM - 7 PM
