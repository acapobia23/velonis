Weekly Tips

THE WEEKEND 19-20th JULY IN FLORENCE

Events, Museums, Markets and Tips



Ars Manualis: in Piazza di Santa Maria Novella you will find the artistic handicraft market during the weekend (H 10/20)

Fierucolina di Santo Spirito: on Sunday in Piazza di Santo Spirito you can buy products of the land and handicrafts (H 9/19)

Museo Diffuso: Want to admire Florence from above and discover hidden viewpoints of the city? You can explore the city’s towers, gates, and fortresses—like Porta San Frediano, Torre della Zecca, Torre San Niccolò, and Fortezza da Basso—to learn more about how Florence has grown and changed over time. (More info)

Forte Belvedere: Built in the late 1500s as a fortress to defend the Oltrarno area, Forte Belvedere is now open with a new exhibition that explores the city’s history and identity through the Firenze Forma Continua project. Open daily 10 AM – 8 PM.


Outdoor Cinema


Apriti Cinema: Enjoy free open-air screenings in the courtyard of Palazzo Pitti every night at 9:45 PM. (Info and full program)

Cinema in Manifattura: In cantiere is a summer series at Manifattura Tabacchi with premieres, cult classics, kids’ movies, documentaries, and films in original languages with Italian subtitles—every evening! (Tickets and program)

Cinema nel Chiostro: Every evening in the courtyard of the former Sant’Orsola convent, enjoy films in their original language in collaboration with Spazio Alfieri. (Tickets and info)

Cinema in Villa: Villa Bardini hosts one of the most scenic outdoor cinemas in Italy, on its amazing terrace overlooking Florence. (More info)


Festivals & Exhibitions


Estate Fiesolana: The famous summer festival at the Roman Theater of Fiesole is back, with music, theater, performances, and concerts in a magical setting. (More info)

Estate Fiorentina: The city is buzzing with thousands of events—music, literature, theater, dance, cinema, and more—all under this year’s motto: How alive the city is! (Info)

Tracey Emin. Sex and Solitude: It's the last weekend of the exhibition at Palazzo Strozzi, offering a journey through key moments in Tracey Emin’s career—from the 1990s to today—exploring powerful themes like the body and desire, love and sacrifice.

Mercantia: The festival is back in Certaldo with street art, aerial performances, games, mimes, circus-style setups, and art installations. (Info)

Musart Festival: Great music echoes through the hills of Florence at the Medici Park in Pratolino, with lots of concerts and live shows. (Info and tickets)

Cerreto Street Wine: This weekend from 6 PM, the historic wine festival takes place in the old town of Cerreto Guidi, with local wine producers and street food.


Summer Hangouts & Live Music


Lumen: A beautiful green space with tons of activities. (Full schedule)

Ultravox: At Le Cascine – perfect for grabbing a drink, some food, and enjoying great live music. (More info)

Instabile: A creative space full of culture in motion, with food, art, theater, and music. (More info)
