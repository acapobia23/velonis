Weekly Tips

The Weekend 13th-14th December in Florence


This weekend Florence shines bright with the Light Festival illuminating its historic monuments,
while Christmas markets fill the piazzas and artisan crafts take center stage across the city.
From ice skating under the stars to hunting for antiques and handmade treasures,
the weekend offers lights, traditions, and creative discoveries.
Get ready to explore, discover, and enjoy!



Firenze Light Festival

Light installations, video mapping and projections on several buildings in the city---
an invitation to enjoy Florence's beauty with care and respect for its heritage.
From Ponte Vecchio to Santa Maria Novella, from Palazzo Vecchio to the illuminated boulevards.



Florence on Ice

The garden at Fortezza da Basso turns into a Christmas Village
with the longest ice-skating rink in Europe and a 55-meter-tall Ferris wheel offering amazing views over the city skyline.



Christmas Market at Piazza Santa Croce

Enjoy the Christmas magic in Piazza Santa Croce with the traditional market.
Every day you'll find stalls with crafts, local products, gift ideas, decorations and food from all over the world.



Creative Mani Factory

A weekend in Piazza dei Ciompi dedicated to the beauty of local, handmade creations.
Around 30 exhibitors from all over Italy will showcase ceramics, textiles, illustrations, jewelry, accessories, and decorations.



Ognissanti in Festa - Saturday

On Saturday in Piazza Ognissanti you'll find a market exhibition of collectibles,
vintage pieces, and creative crafts for a Christmas fully dedicated to local artisans.



The Arttour - Il Bello in Piazza

Piazza Strozzi brings, for the weekend, the best of Florentine and Tuscan artistic and traditional craftsmanship---
from jewelry to ceramics, tailored clothing to fine knitwear, leather goods, hand-woven textiles, artistic mosaics, traditional painting, and artisan printing.



Nataleperfile

At Palazzo Corsini you'll find a charity market with big-name brands,
offering everything from clothing and accessories to beauty, design items, gourmet products, and home goods.



Ars Manualis

The artisan market returns this weekend to Manifattura Tabacchi,
featuring crafts and design, all surrounded by live music and lots of activities.



Indipendenza Antiquaria

This weekend in Piazza Indipendenza you can browse the stands in search of rarities, old objects, and antiques---
and on Sunday, the stalls continue in Piazza Savonarola.



Fiera d'Oltrarno - Sunday

On Sunday in Piazza Santo Spirito, lots of stalls pop up with crafts, antiques, books, clothing,
vintage items, and typical products like honey, truffles, and much more.



Fierucola del Carmine

Grab your groceries in Piazza del Carmine with the producers of the Fierucola---
bread, pasta, veggies, new olive oil, wine, fruit, honey, flours, cheeses, and more.



Apotecha Market

A market exhibition offering top-quality crafts, art, and local products,
spread across the cloisters and indoor spaces of the Santa Caterina monastic complex,
where you'll also find a food & drink area.
