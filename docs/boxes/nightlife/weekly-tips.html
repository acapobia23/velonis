Weekly Tips

The Weekend 8th-9th November in Florence


This weekend Florence celebrates Renaissance masters, explores Belle Époque Paris,
and fills its squares with artisan markets and autumn festivities.
From Beato Angelico to Toulouse-Lautrec, from handcrafted goods to vintage treasures,
the second weekend of November offers a perfect blend of culture, shopping, and seasonal flavors.
Get ready to explore, discover, and enjoy!



Beato Angelico at Palazzo Strozzi

Fondazione Palazzo Strozzi and the Museo di San Marco present Beato Angelico,
an extraordinary exhibition dedicated to Fra Giovanni da Fiesole,
a true symbol of 15th-century Florentine art.



Toulouse-Lautrec at Museo degli Innocenti

The Museo degli Innocenti hosts a new temporary exhibition exploring the life and work
of one of the most iconic artists of the Belle Époque: Henri de Toulouse-Lautrec.
A journey into the Paris of the Belle Époque.



Oceans. David Doubilet at Villa Bardini

Villa Bardini celebrates the work of David Doubilet,
a pioneer of underwater exploration and one of the world's leading underwater photographers.



The Rose That Grew From Concrete

This group exhibition of contemporary art takes place in the former Convent of Sant'Orsola.
Through site-specific works, fourteen artists tell the story of the site's transformations,
symbolically healing its wounds and paying tribute to the strength of nature.



The City of Women at Museo del Novecento

The Museo del Novecento pays tribute to artist Lorenzo Bonechi,
exploring his artistic universe where ethereal and sacred figures meet minimalist landscapes and architecture,
creating a dialogue between spirituality and humanity.



Artist's Brunch - Sunday 9th November

This Sunday, enjoy a brunch by Mercato Centrale
while an art expert shares stories, anecdotes, and fun facts about the life of Beato Angelico.

Info & bookings: info.firenze@mercatocentrale.it



Tuscan Taste at Conventino Caffè Letterario

At the Conventino Caffè Letterario, over 30 producers --- from winemakers and distillers to food artisans ---
will bring you two days full of exceptional flavors:
from craft wines and cured meats to cheese, honey, chocolate, and spirits.



Creative Factory - This Weekend

This weekend in Piazza dei Ciompi, don't miss the event for lovers of independent design,
contemporary crafts, and sustainable creativity.

When: 9:30 AM - 8 PM



Ognissanti in Festa - Saturday 8th November

On Saturday, Piazza Ognissanti hosts a lively market dedicated to creative crafts,
collectibles, and vintage treasures.

When: 9 AM - 8 PM



Autumn Fair - Sunday 9th November

On Sunday, Piazza del Carmine comes alive with a celebration of organic farming,
handmade crafts, local flavors, and live music.

When: 9 AM - 6 PM



Oltrarno Fair - Sunday 9th November

Also on Sunday, the historic market returns to Piazza Santo Spirito,
featuring small antiques and artisanal creations.

When: 9 AM - 7 PM



New Olive Oil Tasting

Celebrate autumn in the most authentic and delicious way --- by tasting the season's freshly pressed olive oil!
You can join the celebrations at "Salunta e Sapori d'Autunno" in Compiobbi,
Olio Verde in Calenzano, or Autumnia in Figline Valdarno.



Food Festivals

The Handmade Tortello Festival in Fiesole;
the Polenta and Autumn Flavours Festival in Sieci;
the White and Black Truffle Festival in Scarperia.
