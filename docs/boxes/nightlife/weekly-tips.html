Weekly Tips




The Weekend 20th-21st December in Florence


This weekend Florence shines bright with the Light Festival illuminating its historic monuments,
while Christmas markets fill the piazzas and creative crafts take center stage across the city.
From ice skating under the stars to hunting for vinyl records and vintage treasures,
the weekend offers lights, traditions, and artistic discoveries.
Get ready to explore, discover, and enjoy!



Firenze Light Festival

Light installations, video mapping and projections on several buildings in the city---
an invitation to enjoy Florence's beauty with care and respect for its heritage.
From Ponte Vecchio to Santa Maria Novella, from Palazzo Vecchio to the illuminated boulevards.



Florence on Ice

The garden at Fortezza da Basso turns into a Christmas Village
with the longest ice-skating rink in Europe and a 55-meter-tall Ferris wheel offering amazing views over the city skyline.



Christmas Market at Piazza Santa Croce

Enjoy the Christmas magic in Piazza Santa Croce with the traditional market.
Every day you'll find stalls with crafts, local products, gift ideas, decorations and food from all over the world.



Christmas Concert - Sunday

Sunday at the Cathedral of the Image, let yourself be moved by traditional Christmas pieces
and beautiful melodies by W. A. Mozart, F. Mendelssohn and G. F. Handel,
performed by the stunning voices of soprano Anastasiia Demchenko and tenor Francesco Fortes.



Comics, Records & Vintage Market

A weekend in Piazza dei Ciompi, the perfect place to hunt for that rare vintage comic,
rediscover hard-to-find vinyl records that made music history,
or add some authentic vintage style to your wardrobe.



La Fierucolina di Natale - Sunday

Sunday in Piazza Ognissanti you'll find a Christmas market dedicated to collectibles,
vintage items and creative craftsmanship, celebrating local artisans.



Ars Manualis

In Piazza Santa Maria Novella, 18 wooden stalls welcome master artisans
for the Christmas edition of this market celebrating traditional skills and craftsmanship.



Plants & Flowers Market - Saturday

Saturday at Manifattura Tabacchi, a full day dedicated to nature, art and handmade creativity,
with exhibitors, talks and workshops.



Indipendenza Antiquaria

Over the weekend in Piazza Indipendenza, browse through stands in search of rare finds, antiques and objects from the past---
on Sunday the market continues in Piazza Savonarola.

A special charity market featuring big names---from fashion and accessories to beauty,

Fiera d'Oltrarno - Sunday

Sunday in Piazza Santo Spirito, with plenty of stalls offering crafts, antiques, books, clothing and vintage finds,
plus local products like honey, truffles and much more.
