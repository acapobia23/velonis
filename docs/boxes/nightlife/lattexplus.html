Lattex Plus

Born in Florence in 2010, this independent collective has become one of Italy's most respected platforms for cutting-edge electronic music, blending underground culture, visual arts, and community spirit.



Known for its immersive events in unconventional spaces — from Renaissance villas to industrial warehouses — Lattex+ brings world-class DJs and producers to Florence. The vibe is inclusive, international, and deeply rooted in artistic experimentation.


Where the dance floor becomes a space for connection.



🎧 Music Style: House, Techno, Experimental

📍 Venues: Vary by event — check their website or Instagram for updates

💡 Tip: Events often sell out — book early!
