Teatro Del Sale


Teatro del Sale offers a unique cultural dining experience where guests enjoy buffet-style Tuscan cuisine while attending live performances, from music to theater. This vibrant space, founded by Fabio Picchi, combines culinary art with entertainment in a warm, community atmosphere.
